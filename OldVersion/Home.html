What is our purpose?

Our purpose is simple, we want to simplifiy learning of speed formulas for rubics cubes, including letter code that comes with it. This may not seem importatn but that simplifiyes understanding of which moves mean which letter. This will help you use these formulas without spending hours trying figure what is what, because we did it for you.
